## How to Battle

### Challenge a Friend
1. Go to a quiz that supports battles
2. Click the "Challenge Friend" button
3. Select a friend from the dropdown list
4. Choose your battle settings (time limit, question count)
5. Click "Send Challenge"
6. Wait for your friend to accept

### Join Matchmaking Queue
1. Go to a quiz that supports battles
2. Click "Quick Battle" or "Join Queue"
3. The system will find an opponent with similar skill level
4. Battle starts automatically when a match is found

### During a Battle
1. **Read the Question**: Take your time to understand
2. **Select Your Answer**: Click on the best option
3. **Time Matters**: Faster correct answers earn bonus points!
4. **Watch Your Progress**: See your score and time remaining
5. **Stay Focused**: No distractions during battle

## Understanding Scores & Ratings

### Points System
- **Base Points**: 10 points for each correct answer
- **Time Bonus**: Extra points for quick answers (up to 5 bonus points)
- **Accuracy Bonus**: Consistent performance rewards

### ELO Rating
- **What is ELO?**: A skill-based rating system (like chess rankings)
- **How it Works**:
  - Win against higher-rated opponents = big rating gain
  - Win against lower-rated opponents = small rating gain
  - Lose to higher-rated opponents = small rating loss
  - Lose to lower-rated opponents = big rating loss

### Battle Results
After each battle, you'll see:
- **Final Score**: Your total points
- **Accuracy**: Percentage of correct answers
- **Time Bonus**: Points earned from speed
- **Rating Change**: How your ELO rating changed
- **New Rank**: Your position on leaderboards

## Leaderboards

### Types of Leaderboards
- **Daily**: Reset every day at midnight
- **Weekly**: Reset every Monday
- **Monthly**: Reset on the 1st of each month
- **All-Time**: Lifetime rankings

### What to Look For
- **Your Rank**: Current position
- **Rating**: Your ELO score
- **Win Rate**: Percentage of battles won
- **Streak**: Current win/loss streak
- **Last Battle**: When you last played

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How do I challenge someone specific?
A: Use the "Challenge Friend" option and select them from the dropdown.

### Q: What happens if I lose connection during a battle?
A: The battle will pause and resume when you reconnect, if time allows.

### Q: Can I play on mobile devices?
A: Yes! The battle system is fully responsive and works on phones and tablets.

### Q: How are ties handled?
A: Ties go to the player with faster average answer time.

### Q: Can I see my opponent's answers?
A: No, answers are private during battle for fair competition.

### Q: What if I think there's a mistake in scoring?
A: Review your battle history and contact support with specific details.
